# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Overbrilliant community a respectful, productive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on ideas, evidence, and tradeoffs.
- Respect maintainers' time and project boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Spam, trolling, or repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Abusive behavior toward maintainers, contributors, or users.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or block users when behavior harms the project or community.

To report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through the contact method listed on the Overbrilliant GitHub organization profile.
